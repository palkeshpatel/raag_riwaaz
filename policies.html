Size Guide

How to Measure

To find your perfect fit, please measure yourself using a soft measuring tape.

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline
  • Hip: Measure around the fullest part of your hips
  • Length: Measure from shoulder to desired length

Care Instructions

General Care

  • Always check the care label before washing
  • Wash similar colors together
  • Use gentle cycle for delicate items
  • Air dry when possible to maintain shape

Fabric Specific Care

  • Cotton: Machine wash cold, tumble dry low
  • Silk: Dry clean only or hand wash cold
  • Wool: Dry clean or hand wash with wool detergent
  • Embroidered Items: Dry clean recommended

Storage Tips

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Use padded hangers for delicate items
  • Fold heavy items to prevent stretching
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
