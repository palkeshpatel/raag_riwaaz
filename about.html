Our Story

Raag & Riwaaz was born in the vibrant streets of London, where the rhythm of modern life meets the timeless traditions of Indian craftsmanship. Our founder, inspired by the rich tapestry of Indian culture, envisioned a fashion label that would honor heritage while embracing contemporary global style.

The name "Raag & Riwaaz" itself tells our story - "Raag" represents the musical traditions that have shaped Indian culture for centuries, while "Riwaaz" signifies the customs and practices passed down through generations. Together, they embody our mission to create clothing that resonates with both tradition and modernity.

From traditional kurtas and sherwanis that have graced royal courts to contemporary hoodies and t-shirts designed for the urban professional, each piece in our collection is a testament to the seamless blend of heritage and innovation.
